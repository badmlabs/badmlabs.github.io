Featherwork — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 July 2026 · Applies to the Featherwork Android app (formerly Badminton Court Simulator)

The short version

Featherwork has no accounts, no ads, and no analytics. Your drills live on your device. The only data that leaves your device is what is strictly required to process and validate subscription purchases.

Data stored on your device

Saved drills, court settings and marker customizations are stored locally on your device. We cannot access them. Uninstalling the app deletes them.

Purchases

Subscriptions (Drill Vault Pro) are processed by Google Play. We never see your payment details. To validate your subscription and unlock premium content, the app shares a purchase token and a randomly generated app-instance identifier with RevenueCat, our subscription-management provider, which processes this data on our behalf. This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or any other personal information.

Shared drill links

When you share a drill, the drill's positions are encoded directly in the link itself — no drill data is uploaded to any server. Link-landing pages are hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect standard access logs as described in GitHub's privacy statement.

What we don't do

  • No user accounts, names, emails or contact lists
  • No location data
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No selling or renting of data — we have nothing to sell

Children

Featherwork is a general-audience training tool and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.
